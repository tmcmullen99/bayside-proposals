The platform's defining feature

The designer stops being a bottleneck.

Every other CRM in this category gates change requests behind designer time. Sales Portal flips the model: the homeowner drives iteration, the designer reviews and approves. Less designer follow-up. Faster decisions. More closed deals.

Currently, a single material change takes 3-5 days: homeowner emails, designer reads, opens CAD, edits, exports new PDF, emails back, waits. With Sales Portal, the homeowner clicks a swatch, picks the alternative, sees the price update — designer reviews and approves in under a minute.

The end-to-end flow

From "lead landed" to "contract signed" — eight steps, one system.

Every step below either happens automatically, in the homeowner's hands, or in the designer's queue. Nothing falls through cracks. Nothing requires a second platform.

1
Lead arrives → designer assigned
Lead is captured from any source (web form, referral, JobNimbus inbox, walk-in). Auto-assigned to the available designer. Pre-consult nurture sequence pauses pending consultation.
Replaces: Typeform + Mailchimp + manual JobNimbus assignment
2
In-person consultation → account created on the spot
Designer arrives at the property. After the walkthrough, opens Sales Portal on a tablet, creates the homeowner account in 60 seconds, sends magic link. Homeowner logs in before the designer leaves.
Replaces: "I'll send you something later" → silence
3
Designer builds the proposal
3 hours instead of 6+. Imports site dimensions, selects materials from the central catalog (pricing auto-populates), writes the section narratives, publishes. 48-hour signing discount countdown auto-starts.
Replaces: CAD + spreadsheet + Word + PDF email
4
Homeowner explores autonomously
Reads sections, compares materials, swaps swatches in real time, forwards to spouse, asks questions in the chat — all without designer involvement. Engagement events stream to the designer's dashboard.
No designer time required
5
Iteration happens at homeowner pace, not designer pace
Material swaps: instant. Redesign requests: structured form, designer reviews in queue. Pricing updates live. Discount countdown stays public, creating urgency without high-pressure sales.
3-5 day cycles → minutes
6
Smart-reply AI keeps the thread warm
When homeowner messages, designer sees 3 AI-drafted replies in Bayside's voice, contextual to the conversation, the bid amount, and the engagement state. Tap → edit a word → send. ~45 seconds per touchpoint instead of ~12 minutes.
Designer follow-up time: down 90%+
7
Sign in browser → JobNimbus job auto-created
Homeowner signs inline on the proposal page. JobNimbus job and invoice fire automatically. Designer notified. Nurture sequence transitions to "signed" status. Discount-window math closes itself out.
Replaces: DocuSign back-and-forth + manual JobNimbus entry
8
War Room stays open during construction
Same chat thread that closed the deal continues during the build. Homeowner asks questions about the install, attaches photos of progress, the designer/PM responds. No second platform.
